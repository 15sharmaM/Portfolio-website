Legality and Security

On this page I will be addressing any legal issues with my website as well as security.

Legality

Plagiarism and Copyright

Since my website is Copyright protected, I have addressed this by adding the Copyright symbol and the relevant year to the footer of each linked website.

The images used throughout my website are free to use and wherever a licence has been indicated, I have repsected it by attributing the relevant author/authors,using the <cite> tags in my html source code.

GDPR and Data Collection

As part of the interaction between the user and my website I created a form in my Contact page so that users may contact me via email.I considered GDPR in this form by ensuring the questions and the data the user could input,was kept to a minimum and relevant.I included a checkbox in my form to allow me to contact them if necessary and that they agreed to this when inputting their email into the form itself.

I'm following GDPR by ensuring that no one else has access to the user's email/data and that the data that is collected,remains within the EEA since I live in the UK and the data isn't being sent to an external website beyond the EEA.

Security

I have considered the Security of my website in the Contact Page form by using <type=email> in my html when asking for the email. This is relevant because the tag has built in email verification and prevents the entry of malicious software .
